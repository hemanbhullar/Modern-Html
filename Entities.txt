What are HTML Entities? List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.

They are not seen on the typical keyboard. They provide a wide range of characters, allowing you to add icons, geometric shapes, mathemathical operators, and so on.

2. greater Than(>) --> Entity Name: &gt, Entity Number: &#62.
3. ampersand(&) --> Entity Name: &amp, Entity Number: &#38.
